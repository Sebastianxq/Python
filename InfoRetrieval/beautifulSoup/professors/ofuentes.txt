Olac Fuentes



Associate Professor in Computer Science· The University of Texas at El Paso· ofuentes@utep.edu

 
 
B.S. degree in Industrial Engineering from the  Instituto Tecnologico de Chihuahua, in Chihuahua, Chihuahua, Mexico.
M.S. degree in Computer Science from the University of Texas at El Paso, in El Paso, Texas, U.S.A.
Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the University of Rochester, in Rochester, New York, U.S.A.


I am interested in Machine Learning and its applications to Computer Vision, Bioinformatics, Scientific Data Analysis, Robotics, and Natural Language Processing. I lead the  Vision and Learning Laboratory (VL-Lab) at UTEP and I am also affiliated with the  Interactive Systems Group (ISG).
